An Improved Car Camping Water Dispenser

I really like my car camping water dispenser. This water dispenser produces a nice steady flow with no batteries. Also, you have 2 choices of sprayer attachments, and the water dispenser is portable. I made the water dispenser using a 0.5 gallon (56oz) weed sprayer bottle, an extra thick straw, marine epoxy, and some tubing with a tube clamp. Here are links to the supplies for making your own water dispenser if you are feeling inspired!
