Healthy Gut Routine

We tend to take our health for granted until something goes wrong. In my case, I was diagnosed with Acute Severe Ulcerative Colitis in February 2023. Ulcerative Colitis is a miserable digestive disease. I was hospitalized twice, and from January - March 2023, I had lost so much weight that I was just skin and bones. Thankfully, during my second hospitalization at the end of March 2023, I received an infusion of Remicade. This medicine worked miraculously on me. Within hours I felt like a dark cloud that had been keeping me down, had just floated away. I was able to recover in just a couple of months. By July 2023, my colon looked almost normal. During my recovery, I also implemented a healthy gut routine. The tables below show my healthy gut routine, which has kept me free from any flares since my first infusion of Remicade. This information might be helpful to someone else who is suffering from Ulcerative Colitis, or anyone who is interested in improving his or her gut health.

UPDATE FEB 2024: My fecal calprotectin level returned to a high level. I strayed from my normal routine during the Christmas and New Years Holidays, and some inflammation returned. Thankfully, my infusion of Inflectra once again 'put out the fire'. And I learned a valuable lesson: I MUST minimize stress, especially during the holidays.


Diet
Breakfast:
Steel Cut Oatmeal with Kefir & Blueberries (Kefir is homemade from raw unpasteurized whole milk)
Lunch:
Kefir Smoothie (Usually made with frozen bananas, frozen strawberries, and frozen blueberries)
Scrambled Eggs (Microwave farmers’ market eggs in Pyrex dish.)
Avocado and mild salsa
Tortillas, Rice, or Farmers’ Market Sourdough Bread
Dinner:
Bison Burgers or Fish
Steamed Vegetables
Avocado and mild salsa
Tortillas, Rice, or Farmers’ market Sourdough Bread


Medicines and Supplements
Mesalamine 2.4 grams/day
Remicade/Inflectra infusions -Every 8 weeks
Vitamin D
Vitamin B
Multivitamin
Fish Oil
Organic Psyllium Husk Powder -mix with a large glass of filtered water. Drink in the afternoon
Chicken Bone Broth -Brodino brand, purchased at farmers’ market. Usually drink with dinner


Lifestyle
Install pur water filter in sink in garage. Store 2 Nalgene bottles of filtered water in refrigerator
Keep a Nalgene bottle with filtered water near me as much as possible
Drink large glass of filtered water first thing in the morning
Total body workout: 3 times/wk, 30 minutes. -But I take very few breaks. It’s a miserable 30 min
Exercise bike: 2 times/wk, 20 minutes. Alternate 90 sec fairly easy, 90 sec difficult, for 20 min
Sundays: Hike Mt Baldy from Manker Flats, Basketball, P90X2 Yoga, or 20 min of Flowfit
Sleep: Usually 7.5 – 8 hrs, and usually 20 min nap when I return home from work
No caffeine
1 or 2 glasses of wine per month maximum. No other alcohol
Go out to eat as little as possible. Usually 1 time per month
